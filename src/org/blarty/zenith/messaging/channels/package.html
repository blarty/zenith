Determines the types of channels available to the messaging system.
The system includes:
  • Point to Point channels
  • Nested channels
  • Publish/Subscribe Channels
  • Wildcard Subscribers
  • Datatype Channels
  • Invalid Message Channels - cannot be processed
  • DLO - Dead Letter Office - Storage for messages that cannot be delivered
  • Guaranteed Delivery
  • Channel Adapters
  • Message Bridges
  • Message Bus
